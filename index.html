Note on muesli recipe — The low-carb muesli contains sunflower seeds (high omega-6) and sliced almonds. To improve the omega ratio: replace sunflower seeds with extra hemp hearts or pumpkin seeds, and use walnuts instead of almonds. Pumpkin seeds and hemp hearts have a far better omega-6:3 ratio than sunflower seeds.
Supplement recommendation — Consider a high-quality fish oil providing 2–3g EPA+DHA daily. For her, liquid fish oil is better absorbed than capsules given the ileum resection. Look for products specifying EPA and DHA amounts, molecularly distilled (low mercury). Discuss with GP. Consider an omega-3 index blood test to baseline current levels.

Her medical priorities — Terminal ileum removed: impaired absorption of B12, fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E, K. Always consume fat-soluble vitamins with dietary fat at the same meal. Ascending colon removed: faster transit — all vegetables cooked soft. 100% coeliac: strictly no gluten in any ingredient. Daily 30 min sun walk is excellent for vitamin D. Discuss B12 injections or sublingual B12 with GP — dietary intake alone is often insufficient post-ileum resection.
